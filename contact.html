Frequently Asked Questions

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your order by clicking on the tracking link in the email or by visiting our order tracking page.

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Items must be unused, with tags attached and in original packaging. Some exclusions may apply. Please see our Returns & Exchanges page for full details.

Currently, we only ship within India. We're working on expanding our shipping options to other countries in the near future.

If you need to change or cancel your order, please contact us immediately at support@nostra.com with your order number. We process orders quickly, so we can only modify orders that haven't been shipped yet.
